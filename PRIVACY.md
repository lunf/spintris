# Privacy Policy

Spintris is a watchOS game that runs locally on your Apple Watch.

## Data Collection

Spintris does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

## Analytics

Spintris does not include analytics, tracking SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party telemetry.

## Network Access

Spintris does not use network features and does not send gameplay data to any server.

## Game Data

Score, level, cleared lines, board state, and current gameplay state are kept in memory while the app is running. They are not uploaded or shared.

## Permissions

Spintris uses Apple Watch haptics for gameplay feedback. It does not request access to location, contacts, health data, microphone, camera, photos, or other personal information.

## Contact

If you have privacy questions about Spintris, contact the app developer through the distribution channel where you obtained the app.
